About Me

I am a multidimensional researcher and developer at the Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST), where I pursue my passion for understanding the fundamental nature of matter while building cutting-edge software solutions.

My work spans the fascinating intersection of Materials Science, Nanotechnology, and Computational Physics, with a focus on functional materials, heterojunction-based photocatalysts, and DFT-assisted molecular modeling for environmental and pharmaceutical applications.

Beyond the laboratory, I am a software engineer inspired by Nikola Tesla's vision, developing blockchain solutions, AI applications, and decentralized systems. I am also a writer, poet, artist, and spiritual practitioner who believes in the unity of science, technology, and consciousness.

As a PhD aspirant, I study structure–property relationships, charge transfer mechanisms, and spectroscopy-supported theoretical insights, engaging in interdisciplinary research and international collaborations.
